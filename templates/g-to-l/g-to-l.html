What is Gram to Liter Conversion?

Gram to liter (g to l) conversion is the process of converting substance weight (measured in grams) into its volume (measured in liters). Since grams measure mass and liters measure volume, the conversion depends on the density of the substance because different substances have different densities. For example, 1000 grams of water is equal to 1 liter because water density is 1 g/ml. But in the same case of oil or flour or any other substance which does not have a density of 1 g/ml, the measurements will be changed.

Formula:

To convert grams to liters, you have to divide the weight by the density of the substance. Note that the density must be in grams per liter (g/l).

If the density is in grams per milliliter (g/ml), then you have to multiply the density by 1000 to convert it into grams per liter (g/l). Here is a basic formula for a substance which has a density in g/ml to make a conversion.

liters = grams / 1,000 √ó density

Example:

Convert 600 grams of milk with a density of 1.03 into liters.

As we know the formula of this conversion, we can solve this example by using the same formula:

  • Liters = 600 / 1000 x 1.03

Where:

Grams of Milk = 600

Density of Milk = 1.03

Answer: 0.58

So, 600 grams of milk equals 0.58 L.

What is Density?

Density is the relationship between mass and volume. It tells us how heavy something is for its size.

Formula Of Density:

Density = Mass / Volume

Units: g/cm¬≥ or g/L

FAQs

Q1: Can we directly convert grams to liters?

+

A: No, direct conversion is not possible. You always need density to correctly convert grams into liters, because every substance has a different density, since grams measure mass and liters measure volume.

Q2: How to convert cooking ingredients from grams to liters?

+

A: To convert cooking ingredients from grams to liters, you have to know the density of each substance. Once you know the density, use the above formula mentioned in the article before to make the conversion.

Q3: What units do grams and liters measure?

+

A: Grams measure mass (weight), while liters measure volume (space).

  • Gram: How heavy something is.
  • Liter: How much space something occupies.
