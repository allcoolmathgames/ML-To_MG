Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is this 1:15 ratio?

-

A: For most standard naturally aspirated engines, this ratio provides a good ballpark estimate. Turbocharged or high-performance engines may produce more power per cc.

Q: Why use 15 as the divisor?

-

A: The 1:15 ratio has become a standard rule of thumb in the automotive industry because it works well for most production engines. It's easy to remember and calculate mentally.

Q: When might this estimate be off?

-

A: The estimate may vary for:

  • Turbocharged or supercharged engines (typically produce more HP per cc)
  • Diesel engines (typically produce less HP per cc)
  • High-performance or racing engines
  • Very small or very large engines
  • Hybrid or electric motors
